How do we know that kidneys are functioning well?

Blood testing

Serum creatinine

  • Creatinine is a surrogate marker that we use to determine if the kidneys are filtering blood well.
  • Creatinine itself does not harm the body; it is a natural product of muscle breakdown.
  • It is rid by the kidney in the form of urine, and is mostly filtered by the glomerulus.

    • Urine testing

    Protein
    • A normal glomerulus will filter blood without allowing an excess of protein leak into the tubules.
    • Albumin is the specific protein kidney doctors look for in the urine.
    • Your pediatrician might refer your child if they detect albumin in a urine sample from a urine dipstick.
